Cloud-Class is a web-application that enables schools and teachers to organise, manage and support the educational activities.

Features

It uses web2 technologies that improve the user experience and make the data transfer faster

It is available 365*24 in several different languages

It supports drag-and-drop

Discussion Forums
Messages
User time table
Exercises automatic correction

Other Important Features

The usage of the "cloud-class" is free of charges for schools, teachers and students

A school may only pay for support and training, in case the how-to-videos are not enough and they need additional training and support
